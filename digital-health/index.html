Why This Matters

Mental health conditions are the leading cause of disability in Canada. Digital technology — when used without intention or limits — is increasingly recognised as a contributing factor, particularly for adolescents aged 12–17. The good news is that targeted individual actions can meaningfully reduce these risks.

The Social Dilemma (2020)

This Netflix documentary interviews the engineers and executives who built social media platforms — and who are now sounding the alarm about what they created.

Key takeaways include:

  • Social media platforms are designed using the same psychological techniques as slot machines.
  • Recommendation algorithms prioritise outrage and anxiety because they drive engagement.
  • Rates of teen depression, anxiety, and self-harm have risen sharply since 2012 — the year smartphones became widespread.
  • Many of the engineers who built these platforms restrict their own children's screen time.

Available on Netflix

The full documentary is available on Netflix. It is recommended for anyone seeking to understand the root causes of digital mental health concerns.
